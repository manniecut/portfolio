Recycl.

After noticing that people who do not have recycling services at their home don't often go out of their way to find alternatives, Angelina Darin (UX/UI designer) and I collaborated on this app. It provides the user maps of local recycling centers as well as the capability to schedule pickups.

EZ Trakr

Being a race drone pilot, I often have a lot of packages in transit, so I created this app to make all tracking information available at a glance. Just enter in your tracking number, carrier, and a label for the package, and it will be added to the list.

GutHub

As I learned to cook for myself more, I found that it's not always easy to come up with ideas day after day. This is an app for people who want a simple way to keep their recipes in one place, and share them with people they might be living or coordinating meals with, to keep everyone on the same page.
